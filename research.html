Research

Zhou Qin is a PhD candidate in Computer Science Department of Rutgers University, his research interests include data-driven investigations with deep learning techniques, with specific focus on spatiotemporal data and related applications in urban computing, user behaviors, human mobility, and recommendation. He obtained his bachelor degree from Harbin Institute of Technology. He is currently under the supervision of Prof. Desheng Zhang.

Project 1: Cellular networks with signaling and subscription data

Project 2: Nation-scale mobile bus WiFi system with diverse log data
