About Me

Zhou Qin is an Applied Scientist at Amazon. Previously, he obtained his Ph.D. degree in Computer Science Department of Rutgers University, under the supervision of Prof. Desheng Zhang.

. He also obtained his bachelor degree of Electrical and Electronic Engineering from Harbin Institute of Technology (# 2 worldwide USNews). His research interests include on data-driven investigations by machine learning and data mining in urban computing and e-commerce settings, e.g., large-scale data and applications such as user behaviors modeling, time series prediction, ranking by reinforcement learning, and anomaly detection.
